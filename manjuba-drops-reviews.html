Your Results Will Vary

This is the most important point. Manjuba Drops works for the vast majority of users, but "works" means different things to different people. A 45-year-old who's mostly healthy and just wants more energy might notice results in a week. A 65-year-old with significant prostate issues and poor sleep habits might need the full three months to see meaningful changes.

Factors that influence results include:
