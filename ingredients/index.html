The Three Systems

Manjuba Drops targets three biological systems that work together to determine male vitality. When you understand these systems, you can see why each ingredient was chosen and how they complement each other.

Vascular System

Blood flow is the foundation of male performance. Two ingredients in the formula — epimedium and alpha lipoic acid — support the nitric oxide pathway and protect blood vessel walls.

Hormonal System

Epimedium Extract

200mg Vascular

Also called horny goat weed, epimedium is the primary blood flow ingredient in the formula. Tribulus Terrestris

200mg Hormonal

Tribulus terrestris is a plant native to warm regions of Europe, Asia, and Africa. It has been used in Ayurvedic and Chinese medicine for thousands of years, primarily for male reproductive health. The fruit, root, and leaves all contain active compounds, but the most studied group is called steroidal saponins, specifically protodioscin.

Research suggests tribulus may support testosterone levels indirectly by influencing luteinizing hormone (LH), which signals the testes to produce testosterone. A 2014 study in the Journal of the International Society of Sports Nutrition found that 750mg of tribulus daily for 30 days increased free testosterone by 16% in men. Saw Palmetto

175mg Protective

Saw palmetto comes from the berries of the Serenoa repens plant, native to the southeastern United States. It is one of the most widely used herbal supplements for prostate health in Europe, where doctors regularly recommend it as a first-line treatment for benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) before turning to pharmaceuticals.

The primary mechanism is inhibition of 5-alpha reductase, the enzyme that converts testosterone to dihydrotestosterone (DHT). Excess DHT is linked to prostate enlargement and hair loss. A Cochrane review of 32 studies found that saw palmetto reduced urinary symptoms in men with BPH, with improvements comparable to finasteride in several trials. At 175mg, Manjuba Drops uses a dose consistent with clinical studies.

Fenugreek Seed Extract

200mg Hormonal

Fenugreek is a plant native to the Mediterranean and parts of Asia. The seeds contain compounds called furostanolic saponins, which researchers have studied for their effects on testosterone and sexual function. It has a long history in both cooking and traditional medicine.

A 2020 systematic review in Complementary Therapies in Medicine analyzed seven randomized controlled trials and concluded that fenugreek supplementation had a significant positive effect on sexual function in men. The proposed mechanism involves inhibiting aromatase (the enzyme that converts testosterone to estrogen) and sex hormone-binding globulin (SHBG), which keeps more free testosterone available for use. Most studies used 500-600mg daily of a concentrated extract.

Mucuna Pruriens

200mg Hormonal

Mucuna pruriens, also known as velvet bean, is a tropical legume native to Africa and tropical Asia. It is one of the few natural sources of L-DOPA, the direct precursor to dopamine. Dopamine is the neurotransmitter most closely associated with motivation, reward, and sexual desire.

A 2012 study in Fertility and Sterility gave 500mg of mucuna pruriens to infertile men for three months. The treatment group saw significant increases in testosterone (+27%) and dopamine levels (+32%), along with improvements in sperm count and motility. The dopamine connection matters because dopamine directly influences libido and sexual function. Low dopamine is associated with reduced desire and difficulty with arousal.

Alpha Lipoic Acid (ALA)

150mg Protective

Alpha lipoic acid is a naturally occurring antioxidant that the body produces in small amounts. It is unique because it is both water-soluble and fat-soluble, meaning it works throughout the entire body. ALA is often called the "universal antioxidant" because it can regenerate other antioxidants like vitamin C, vitamin E, and glutathione after they have been used.

For male health, ALA does two important things. It protects blood vessels from oxidative damage, which directly supports healthy blood flow. And it enhances nitric oxide production, which pairs with epimedium to improve vascular function through a separate pathway. A 2009 study in the journal Urology found that ALA combined with L-carnitine improved erectile function scores in men with mild ED. Why Liquid Format Matters

You could take all seven of these ingredients in capsule form. Many supplements do exactly that. The reason Manjuba Drops uses a liquid format comes down to absorption speed and consistency.

Capsules have to go through several steps before your body can use the ingredients inside. The gelatin or cellulose shell has to dissolve, the powder has to be released, then stomach acid and enzymes have to break it down further. The rate at which all this happens depends on your stomach contents, digestive speed, and individual metabolism. Some days you absorb 40% of a capsule. Other days you absorb 70%. It varies.

A liquid formula skips the capsule dissolution step entirely. When taken sublingually (under the tongue), a portion of the active ingredients is absorbed directly through the mucous membranes into the bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system altogether. Even when swallowed, the liquid is already dissolved, so the ingredients are available for absorption immediately.

For a formula like Manjuba Drops, where the ingredients need to reach specific tissues — blood vessel walls, prostate tissue, hormone receptors — faster absorption means the compounds reach their target sites sooner and at more consistent concentrations. That translates to more reliable results from day to day.

Liquid vs. Capsules: The Practical Difference

  • ✓ Faster onset: Liquid ingredients reach the bloodstream in minutes, not the 30-60 minutes typical for capsules.
  • ✓ Higher bioavailability: Sublingual absorption bypasses first-pass metabolism in the liver, meaning more of the active compound reaches your tissues.
  • ✓ Consistent dosing: No variation based on stomach contents or digestive speed.
  • ✓ Easier to take: No pills to swallow. Just drop the liquid under your tongue or mix with a small amount of water.
